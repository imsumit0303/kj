HOW IT WORKS?

PAY MERCHANT

Enter the merchant’s UPI ID or scan the UPI QR code.

Enter the amount you have to pay.

Select the account from which you want to pay.

Enter your UPI PIN on the NPCI page to complete the payment.

Alternatively, merchant can send you a collect request & you can complete the payment entering your UPI PIN.

SEND MONEY

Enter the UPI ID of the friend to whom you want to send money.

Enter the amount you want to send.

Select the account from which you want to pay.

Enter your UPI PIN on the NPCI page to complete the payment.

Your friend receives the money directly in his bank account and he is instantly notified on his mobile.

COLLECT REQUEST

Enter the VPA of the friend from whom you wish to request money.

Enter the amount you want to request.

Select the VPA/Account in which you want to receive the money.

Your friend instantly receives the notification on his UPI app.

Your friend accepts the request & enters his UPI PIN. You'll instantly receive the amount in your bank account.

UPI IS SAFE & SECURE

DEVICE AUTHENTICATION

When you register on any UPI app, your mobile device details & mobile number are captured through a SMS & registered on the banks servers. This serves as the first factor authentication for UPI.

UPI PIN AUTHENTICATION

Your bank account UPI PIN acts as the 2nd factor authentication. You can change or reset your UPI PIN anytime. You need to enter your UPI PIN on the NPCI page every time you make a payment.

WHY UPI?

UPI vs NETBANKING

No hassle of logging into internet banking using your username & password.

No need to share and ask complicated account numbers and IFSC codes.

No need to add account details and wait for a day to start sending money.

No compulsion to use your bank platform. You can use any UPI app for transferring money.

You can only send money with internet banking, but with UPI you can also request money & do much more.

UPI vs WALLETS

No hassle of adding money to your wallet before you can start transacting.

With UPI, you can pay money to anyone directly from your bank account.

Using a wallet, you can transfer money amongst the users of the same wallet.

Money kept in the wallets doesn’t earn any interest, but with UPI money stays in your bank account & earns interest.

There is a monthly spending limit in the case of wallets. But with UPI, there are no such limits & restrictions.

UPI vs CARDS

No hassle of entering card numbers, expiry date, cvv while making a payment.

No need to wait for OTP & read it from text message to make a payment.

No need to provide sensitive card details and then worry about their misuse.

Card payments are generally used for merchant payments, but with UPI you can also pay your friends.

UPI architecture and the payments flows are such that they make payments faster, simpler & much more secure.
